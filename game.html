Recycle Dude Game

Green Team has created an online game intending to reach wide audiences to spread the message. Landfills present long-term threats to groundwater, surface water, and, most of all, our earth. Unfortunately, it involves a combination of indirect factors that make it hard for people to get motivated to avoid the threats. We believe we could change it by addressing the importance of it in small things like our game. This game would be like a Mario but in a green version addressing the importance of the 3 Râ€™s (recycle, reuse, and reduce). The player will be collecting waste and will be reminded of recycling. Even if a child skips a chapter of the landfilling in the school, it will be one of the thousands of attempts to remind them of its importance.
